  • Erica Andrew ( Born October 11, 1990), better known by her stage name Love Moor, is an American singer-songwriter. She was born in Brooklyn, NY, grew up in Miami , Fl , and later relocated to Birmingham, Al. Love Moor released her first extended play (EP), titled Blu Polka Dots, in the summer of 2015. Love Moor, through great vibes and beautiful energy became the first lady of Loner Dreamer Lover Rebel (LDLR) in 2014.

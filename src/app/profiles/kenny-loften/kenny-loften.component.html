Kenny Loften Truth For The Youth
Basic Info

Kenny Loften is an emerging artist in the underground scene here in Birmingham. However, he has made quite a splash ever since he announced his arrival. His first show he ever booked was just over a year ago, at Slossfest. Imagine that being your first live show! As the younger brother of local spitting legend, Haru, Kenny knows he has a high bar set for him. He's ready to meet the challange.
